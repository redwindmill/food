FRIED SOY GLAZED SPAM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**GLAZE**
2 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp packed dark brown sugar
1/2 to 1 tsp sake

Mix together well in bowl and set to the side

**FRY**
1-3 tsp olive oil or coconut oil
1 12oz can of SPAM

Heat pan at med to high heat with oil and cut spam into even slices (8-10).
Fry until brown and crispy on both sides (3 min).

**GLAZE IN PAN**
Turn to low heat, pour glaze in.
Evenly coat spam slices (turn frequently).
Don't stop until glaze is cooked down and don't burn the sugar
