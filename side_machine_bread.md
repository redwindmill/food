WIP:: BREAD MAKER WHITE BREAD
================================================================================
BASIC (HIGH ELEVATION)
- 1 cup water (1/2 cup HE)
- 1/2 cup whole milk (1 cup HE)
- 4 1/4 cups all purpose flour (add 1tbsp for HE)
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 1 1/2 tsp salt (add 1/4 tsp for HE)
- 2 tsp active dry yeast
- 4 tbsp butter
