PORKOLT STEW
================================================================================

INGREDIENTS:
- 5-6 slices of thick cut bacon, cut into short strips
- 2 cups diced yellow onion (about 1 large)
- 1.5 cups diced green bell pepper (about 1 large)
- 2 cups large diced tomato (about 1 large tomato)
- 1 tbsp pressed garlic (about 3-4 cloves)
- (optional) 2-3 tsp olive oil
- 2lbs chuck beef cubed into 1/2
- 4 tbsp paprika
- 0.5 tsp caraway seed or fennel seed
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp black pepper
- 1-2 bay leafs

FRY:
- Place bacon in a cast iron dutch oven or large pot
- Fry bacon until enough grease is in the bottom
- Put onions in pot, optionally add olive oil
- After sweating onions in bacon, add bell pepper
- After cooking for a few minutes, add garlic
- After cooking for a few minutes, add beef chunks, brown / sear for a bit

MIX:
- Pull off burner, add paprika and mix in well
- Add in caraway / fennel, salt and black pepper; mix in well
- Mix in tomato and add enough water to leave some meat exposed
- Add in bay leaf(s)

STEW:
- Return to burner, bring to a boil
- Cover and simmer for 90 to 120 minutes

Serve with baked potatoes (baked with olive oil, salt and pepper)
