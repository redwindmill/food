WIP:: MAC SALAD
================================================================================

8oz elbow macaroni

2 tbsp grated white / yellow onion
1.5 cups real mayonnaise
2 tbps bread & butter (sweet) pickle juice
3 tbps sweet relish (just chop up pickle chips)
0.25 cups coarsely grates carrots
salt and pepper to taste

Steps:
- Cook mac in pot (with salt)
- mix everything else well in a separate bowel
- mix in mac
- salt and pepper to taste
