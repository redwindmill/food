## Modified Boyardee’s Spaghetti
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**STEW**
1 cup diced onions
1/2 cup grated carrot
6 tbsp olive oil
0.75 tsp salt for onions

1 lb ground beef
1 tsp ground pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
0.75 tsp salt for beef

2 14.5 cans of diced tomatoes; blended
1/2 cup sliced bella mushroom
1 tsp dry basil

*** COOK ***
1) In pot on medium heat, add olive oil
2) Add in onions and salt, cook for about 3 minutes
3) Add in carrots, cook for another 5 minutes
4) Add in meat, salt, pepper, garlic powder, cook until browned
5) Add in tomatoes, mushrooms, basil, cook for 40-45 min

**PASTA**
- 8 oz dry spaghetti pasta
- salt for water
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp grated parmesan cheese

*** COOK ***
1) cook pasta per directions with salt; drain and return to pot
2) Melt and mix butter into noodles
3) melt and mix cheese into noodles
