WAFFLES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**DRY**
- 2 cups bread flour
- 0.75 tsp salt
- 2 tsp active dry yeast
-
**WET**
- 1.5 cups lukewarm milk
- 7 tbsp melted butter
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs
- 2-4 tbsp maple syrup

**STEPS**
- Mix all together
- Let sit for 1.5 hours
