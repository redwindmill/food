WAFFLES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**DRY**
- 2 cups bread flour
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 2 tsp active dry yeast
-
**WET**
- 1 1/2 cups lukewarm milk
- 8 tbsp melted butter (1 stick)
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs
- 2-4 tbsp maple syrup

**STEPS**
- Mix all together
- Let sit for 1 1/2 hours
