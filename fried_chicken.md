# Fried Chicken

## Brine
- 1 cup pickle juice
- 1 1/2 cups buttermilk (or 1 1/2 cups milk + 5 tsp lemon juice)
- 5 tsp soy sauce (or 1 tsp salt)
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp old bay
- 1 large egg
- 3 lbs chicken

Whisk together ingredients and pour into zip lock bag. Add chicken. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours up to overnight.

## Bread
- 1 cup flour
- 1 tbsp cornstarch (2 tbsp for crispy)
- 1 tbsp old bay
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp dried milk (optional)
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

## Prep
- Bread mix

Mix breading together in one bowl. Mix two to three eggs in another bowl. Bread, egg wash, bread.

## Cooking
- 2 inches of oil (refined coconut)
- Heat oil to 350-375°F

Fry 8-10 minutes or until golden and done. Turn to cook evenly.
