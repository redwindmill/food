# SHOYU CHICKEN

Meat:
- Around 5lbs chicken breast
- Salt (to taste)
- Lemon Juice (around 2 tsp)
- Milk (around 1/2 cup)

Hot Marinade:
- 1.75 cups soy sauce
- 5 tbsp honey
- 1.25 cups sugar
- 3 tbsp molasses
- 5 tbsp grated ginger
- 3 tbsp pressed garlic
- 1 1/2 yellow onions, quartered

Sauce:
- 3/4 cup hot marinade
- 4 tbsp corn starch 

PREP MEAT:
- Take chicken breast and beat it flat a bit
- Rub a bit of lemon juice and salt on each piece as you go
- Put all chicken in a mixing bowl; Pour enough milk until it is almost covered
- Place in fridge for at least 30-45 min

STEW:
- in large pot mix in Hot marinade ingredients on medium heat (no onions
- place chicken in pot, cover well
- put onions in and bring to a boil
- Cover pot with a lid and let simmer for 30 min
- Preheat oven to 425 °F
- Every 30 min turn chicken over check tenderness with a fork (easy to insert)
- Remove chicken and place into a pan

BROIL:
- Mix ingredients for sauce until smooth, add back to pot
- Cook for another 5 min on low/med heat until sauce is thickened
- Put chicken in oven to darken a bit (no more than 2 min)

Serve:
- Pour sauce on chicken
