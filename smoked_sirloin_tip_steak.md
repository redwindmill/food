# SMOKED SIRLOIN TIP STEAK

## MEAT
- 1 lb sirloin tip steak (thin)

## MARINADE
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 5 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp black pepper
- salt

## PREP
1. Generously salt both sides of the sirloin tip steak and let it sit at room temperature for 15 minutes.
2. Submerge the steak in the marinade for 6 hours, rotating the pieces twice during that time.

## SMOKING
1. Smoke the steak at 180-200°F for 3 hours without opening the smoker.
2. Raise the smoker temperature to 275-300°F for a faster cook or 225-250°F for a slower cook until the internal temperature reaches 165-170°F.
3. Spray or mop the steak with apple cider vinegar every 30 minutes until the smoke phase is complete.

## COOKING
1. Before wrapping, pour honey and melted butter generously over the top of the steak.
2. Once the internal temperature reaches approximately 170°F, wrap the steak tightly in butcher paper with the fat side sealed inside.
3. Return the wrapped steak to the smoker at 300-325°F and cook until the internal temperature reaches 195-200°F and a probe slides in smoothly with little resistance.

## RESTING
1. Remove the steak from the smoker and rest it in a cooler or insulated container for 1 hour before slicing.
