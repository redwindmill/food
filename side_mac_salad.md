# MAC SALAD

- 2 cups elbow macaroni (about 8oz)
- 2 tbsp grated white / yellow onion
- 1 1/2 cups real mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp bread & butter (sweet) pickle juice
- 3 tbsp sweet relish (just chop up pickle chips)
- 1/4 cup coarsely grates carrots
- salt and pepper to taste

Steps:
- Cook mac in pot (with salt)
- mix everything else well in a separate bowl
- mix in mac
- salt and pepper to taste
