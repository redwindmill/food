## FRIED CHICKEN (JAPANESE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**Meat prep**
- 1-2 lbs butterflied chicken
- aggressive salt and pepper applied to chicken

**Marinade**
- 1/3 cup sake
- 4 tbsp brown sugar

**Batter**
- flour bowl / plate (1/2 cup)
- egg bowl / plate (1 egg)(mixed well)
- Panko bowl / plate (1/2 cup)

**STEPS**
- Soak in marinade for 1 hour
- dredge in flour
- dredge in egg
- dredge in panko crumbs
- fry in shallow oil of your choice

**FAKE TONKATSU SAUCE**
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tsp sake
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 tsp ginger
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
