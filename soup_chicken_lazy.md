LAZY CHICKEN SOUP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BROTH
- 8 cups water
- chicken bullion for 8 cups
- 3 tbsp olive oul
- 1.25 tsp parsley
- 1.25 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp lawry's salt
- 1/2 tsp thyme
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 2 pay leafs

VEGGIES
- 1.5 cups carrots, chopped
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 yellow onion
- 2 yellow potatoes

FINISHING TOUCH
- 1 14oz can of italian diced tomatoes
- 2 tbsp flour

MEAT
- 1 to 2 lbs
- poldka kielbasa sausage (14oz)

PREP
- combine broth and veggies, cook until potatoes are soft
- fry sausage in pan, cook chicken in oven
- when veggies and broth are ready, add meat, tomatoes and flour
- simmer for another 10min
