INSTA POT CHICKEN
================================================================================

RUB
- 2 tsp dry thyme
- 1 tsp dry parsley
- 1 tsp dry oregano
- 1.5 tsp lawry salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 5 tbsp olive oil
- 1 large lemon (juice and zest)

Mix all together and rub on the outside of the bird

BIRD (or POT)
- 1 apple sliced
- 1/2 large onion sliced

POT
- 1 lemon, sliced
- 1 cup water
- 2 tsp chicken bullion

SETTINGS
- 8min per pound raw
- 13min per pound frozen
- Manual, High
