## BEEF SOUP (CROCK POT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**STEW**
- 2 lbs stew beef (chucked)
- 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp all spice
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 3 cups chopped carrots (about 14oz)
- 4 cups chopped yellow potatoes (about 20oz)
- 2 cups water or beef stock
- 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
- salt and pepper for the beef

**COOK**
- Brown meat with oil, salt and pepper (to taste)
- All in crock pot (except flour)
- Set to stew setting

**FINISH**
- Mix in flour to thicken stew
- add in 1/4 cup water or beef stock if needed
