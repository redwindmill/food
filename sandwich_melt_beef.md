# CLASSIC PATTY MELT

## MEAT
- 1 lb ground beef (ground chuck 80/20)
- 2/3 cup chopped onion
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp crushed red pepper or pepper flakes
- pepper to taste
- 5 slices American cheese

## SAUCE
- 2 tbsp mayo
- 2 tbsp ketchup
- 1 tbsp mustard
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

## SANDWICH
- mayo
- butter
- sandwich bread

## STEPS
1. Pan fry onions in butter until caramelized; set aside.
2. Cook meat in same pan, breaking into small chunks.
3. When cooked, drain half the fat; return to burner.
4. Add soy sauce, onion powder, garlic powder, and pepper.
5. Cook until most soy sauce evaporates.
6. Add caramelized onions back in.
7. Add cheese to top; mix well.
8. Mix sauce ingredients together.
9. Coat outside of bread with light mayo.
10. Heat butter in pan; place bread in pan.
11. Place meat mix on one slice; coat with sauce.
12. Assemble and toast.
