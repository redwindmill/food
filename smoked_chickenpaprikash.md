CHICKEN PAPRIKASH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

MEAT:
- 4-5 lbs chicken (thighs)
- 1tbsp salt

VEGGIES:
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cups finely chopped yellow onion (about 1-2 bulbs)
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp smoked paprika

STEW:
- 2 cups chicen stock

FINISH:
- 1.5 cups sour cream

PREP (MEAT)
- Dry pat chicken; apply salt to both sides

PREP (VEGGIES)
- heat up olive oil
- Add onions and salt
- Fry onions until they are clearish and golden
- Pull off heat, mix in paprika

STEW
- Add in chicken, turning to coat in mix
- Add in chicken stock; simmer
- every 10 min flip chicken
- simmer for about 30min

FINISH:
- mix sour cream and flour
- add a bit of the sauce from the pan to sour cream; mix
- mix all together in pan
- gently simmer for another 20 min
