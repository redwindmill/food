WIP:: CORN MUFFINS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**MUFFINS**
- 1 cup all purpose flour
- 1 cup corn meal
- 0.25 cups brown sugar sugar (packed)
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1 tsp cream of tartar
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 1 cup buttermilk (or 1 cup milk + 1 tbsp lemon juice)
- 6(8) tbsp melted butter cooled
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup canned corn (drained)(10oz)

*COOK*
- Set oven to 415F; butter or oil pan / skillet (was 400)
- Cook for 16min (light golden brown on top) (was 20)
