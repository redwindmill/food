## TANG PIE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**INGREDIENTS**
- 10in graham pie crust
- 1/2 cup TANG
- 8oz cream cheese (soft)
- 14oz Sweetened Condensed Milk
- 10oz Whipped cream (topping)

**MIXER**
- put in cream cheese and tang. Mix until mostly uniform
- add in sweetened condensed milk. Mix until uniform (2min)
- add in whipped cream; Mix for another 30-45 seconds

Add to pie pan chill overnight
