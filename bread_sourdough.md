WIP:: SOUR DOUGH OVEN SPRING
================================================================================

**SOURDOUGH**

BY WEIGHT
---------
100% BREAD FLOUR
70% WATER
20% SOUR DOUGH STARTER
2% SALT

BY VOLUME
---------
4 1/3 cup bread flour
1.5 cups water (maybe a bit more)
105g sourdough
2 tsp salt

**YEAST**

BY VOLUME
---------
4 1/3 cup bread flour
1.75 cups water (maybe a bit more)
1 tsp yeast
3 tsp salt

MIX
- Mix all ingredients; let sit for a hour

REST
- pull out fold a few times and tighten into a ball
- let rest for around 4 hours (double in size)

SHAPE
- don't de-gas the dough too much
- fold long ways twice (like a pamphlets)
- Roll like a sushi roll
- tuck ends
- place in pan or basket upside down (seam up)

RISE
- place in fridge for 24 hours

COOK
- preheat oven and pan /w top to 425 F
- put some flour in pan bottom, place dough in pan
- score top, put in oven for 18min
- pull out, remove pan top

CRISP
- cook for 15 more min at same temp
