LAZY CHILI
================================================================================

2 Mcormick 1.25 oz original chili packets
2 lbs ground beef
0.25 or 0.5 cups bacon
1 14.5 oz can of diced tomatoes
2 8oz cans of tomato sauce
2 cans kidney beans
2 bell peppers (or equivalent)
1 medium white onion
1 tbsp olive oil

sharp chedder cheese
salt and pepper to taste

Meat
- Brown meat with a bit of salt a pepper (to taste)
- Cook bacon, cut into small chunks

Pot
- dice bell peppers
- add olive oil, sweat bell peppers in the pot
- add browned meat, bacon
- add tomatoes, beans, seasoning
- cook for 15 min

Toppings
- dice onions
- shred cheddar cheese
