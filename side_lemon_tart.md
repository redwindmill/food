## LEMON TART
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**INGREDIENTS**
- 1 9in gram pie crust
- 3 whole eggs
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 zest from 1 large lemon
- 1/2 cup lemon juice
- 2 tbsp heavy whipping cream
- 1/2 cup butter (1 stick)

**COOK**
- Mix all together and pour in pie pan
- In a heat safe mixing bowl, add eggs, sugar, lemon, cream and butter (chunked)
- Over a large pan of water heat up mixture in bowl
- Whisk until thick (about 10 min)
- Pull off stove, whisk for another 5 minutes to make fluffy
- Pour into pie tin, set for 10min (uncovered)
- Put into fridge for 4 hours

- Cook at 375 for 45-60min, at 45 min check every 10 min
- Edges should be set while the center giggles a bit
- let it set until it completely cools
