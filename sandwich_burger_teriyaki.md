## Teriyaki Burger
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**Ingredients**
- 1 lb ground beef (ground chuck 80/20)
- 1/4 cup Teriyaki (Kikkoman original)
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp Panko (Kikkoman)
- Onion slices
- Bell peppers
- Pineapple slices

**Meat**
- Mix sauce, egg and panko in a large bowl; let stand for 10min
- Mix in ground beef thoroughly.
- Marinate 1-2 hours

**Veggies**
- Saute onions and bell peppers in oil and 1 tbsp teriyaki sauce

**Cooking**
- Grill burger patties
- Grill pineapple
- Toast buns
