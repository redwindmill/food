WIP:: CLASSIC CHEESESTEAK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**Veggies**
- 1 bell pepper
- 1/2 large onion
- 1/2 tsp Lawry's salt
- 1/2 tsp Black pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil

**Meat**
- 1lb shredded beef
- 1-2 tbsp  worcestershire sauce
- 1-2 tsp salt
- 1-3 tsp garlic powder
- 1-3 tsp black pepper
- 1-3 tsp olive oil (as needed)

**Cheese**
- 2 cups mozzarella
- parmesan to taste

**Hoggies**
- Sausage Rolls
- Butter

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Cook Veggies
- On high heat, fry up the veggie ingredients in a pan until soft
- Remove veggies from pan

Cook Meat
- In the same pan on medium heat, cook the meet ingredients until brown
- Unless you've cooked it before don't add all the spices yet

Combine
- Add veggies to the pan
- Season to taste (salt + pepper + worcestershire)
- Add cheese, mix until melted

Bread
- Butter the bread and toast in a pan
- Add cheesesteak mix
