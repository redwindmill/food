BEEF FAJITAS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Marinate:
- 1 cup olive oil
- 2 to 3 tsp Soy Sauce
- 2 to 3 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 heaping tsp crushed red pepper
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Meat:
- 4 lbs beef skirt steak cut into strips

Prep:
- Rest meat in fridge 1 to 8 hours

Veggies (cook separately):
- 1 green bell pepper cut into strips
- 1 red bell pepper cut into strips
- 1 to 2 onions cut into strips

Cook (meat):
- Pour it all into a large pan on medium low
- Cook until strips are cooked through and tender
- Drain liquid from pan and discard
- Raise heat to high to deeply brown and scrips the steak
